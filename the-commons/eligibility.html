Eligibility

Physical Presence in the U.S.

  • You must be physically present in the U.S during the application process.

U.S. Bank Account

  • You must have a bank account at a U.S. based bank or credit union.

U.S. Phone Number

  • You must have a phone number from a U.S based carrier.

U.S. Address

  • You must have a U.S. address.

Valid Visa

  • You must a valid visa for at least 6 months.
