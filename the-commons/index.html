At New Chapter Credit Union, we invite new immigrants to apply for credit even without an SSN. We understand that it takes time to build a credit history and new immigrants can apply even a without credit history. Your creditworthiness will be evaluated by another form of metrics. You can build your credit history by making your payments on time. People new to the U.S. should have equal opportunity to secure a credit to build their credit history.
